About The Chamber

Greetings and welcome to the Legacy Chamber of Commerce, your gateway to the vibrant business community in our city. As a dynamic hub for local businesses, entrepreneurs, and professionals, we are committed to fostering growth, collaboration, and prosperity.

Why choose Legacy Chamber of Commerce

  • Networking Opportunities

    • Connect with like-minded professionals, attend exclusive events, and build valuable relationships within our extensive business network.

  • Resources for Success

    • Access a wealth of resources, from business development tools to educational workshops, designed to help you thrive in today's competitive landscape.

  • Advocacy and Support

    • Benefit from our advocacy efforts as we work tirelessly to represent the interests of our members and contribute to a business-friendly environment.

Events

Attend Ghana's largest Small Business Conferences, Trade Shows & Networking Events for Small Business Owners, Entrepreneurs and Start-Ups

Advocacy Policy

We call upon policymakers, local government officials, and the broader community to support the implementation of targeted tax incentives as a strategic tool for promoting local economic development. By creating a business-friendly environment, we can attract and retain businesses, stimulate job growth, and enhance the overall economic vitality of our community.
