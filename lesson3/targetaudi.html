Target Audience

Our chamber of commerce caters to the following target audience:

  • Local business owners and entrepreneurs
  • Community members interested in supporting local businesses
  • Professionals seeking networking opportunities

Personels

Persona 1: Aaron Billgate

Aaron is a small business owner running a boutique clothing store. He is looking for ways to connect with other local business owners and promote his store to a wider audience.

Persona 2: Priscilla Holland

Priscilla is a young professional who recently moved to the area. She is interested in networking events and mentorship opportunities to expand her professional connections and grow her career.

Scenarios

  1. Priscilla is looking for a mentor in the industry and wants to find out if the chamber offers a mentorship program.
  2. Aaron wants to attend a chamber networking event to meet other business owners and potentially collaborate on marketing initiatives.
  3. A local resident wants to find a directory of all the chamber member businesses to support local shopping.
  4. Aaron is interested in the chamber's resources for improving social media marketing strategies.
  5. Mary wants to learn about upcoming workshops or seminars organized by the chamber that can help him enhance his professional skills.
